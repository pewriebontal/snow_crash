In a dystopian future California, where the U.S. government has abandoned its functions to private franchise corporations, the landscape is dotted with autonomous quasi-national city-state suburbs. 

Hiro Protagonist (The Player - P) is the Deliverator, delivering pizza for the Mafia. A brilliant hacker and sword-fighter, Hiro is on a run with a critically late pizza, but he wrecks his car trying to cut through a heavily fortified neighborhood. 

Now, he finds himself on foot, trapped in a labyrinth of gated franchise borders and corporate barricades (Walls - 1). The crash has scattered his critically late pizza boxes and highly sought-after Snow Crash data capsules (Collectibles - C) all across the grid.

To avoid bringing down the deadly wrath of the Mafia upon himself, and to secure the dangerous Snow Crash virus, Hiro must cautiously navigate this suburban maze. He has to weave through the grid and collect every single scattered pizza and data capsule. 

Only when every last item is collected will the secure gates of the enclave permanently unlock, revealing the final drop-off point (Exit - E). 

Waiting at the exit is a 15-year-old courier named Y.T. on her smart-wheel skateboard, ready to take the hand-off and help him escape the private security forces. The Metaverse and the battle against Raven and L. Bob Rife await, but first, the Deliverator must complete his pizza run!
